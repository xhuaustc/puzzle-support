Gleamory - Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Gleamory ("the App") is a sliding puzzle game developed by Xiaohua Pan. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • Photos you select stay on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Game settings, best records, and saved images are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and the app sandbox).
  • We do not require user accounts, sign-in, or registration.

2. Photos & Camera

The App requests access to your Photo Library and Camera solely to let you choose or take photos for use as puzzle images. These photos are processed entirely on-device. We never upload, share, or access your photos outside the App.

3. Advertising

The free version of the App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. The App requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) authorization before enabling personalized ads. If you decline, only non-personalized ads are shown. Purchasing the Pro upgrade removes all advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Pro Unlock") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — ad serving (free version only). See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. No data is collected from any user regardless of age.

7. Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at xhuaustc@gmail.com.
